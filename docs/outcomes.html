NLSY97 vs Public Benchmarks

Earnings Context: NLSY97 vs Public Surveys

NLSY97 is unweighted and cohort-specific (born 1980–84, ages 37–41). ACS/CPS are weighted population estimates for ages 25–54.

Event-Time Outcomes (Post-Exit)

Preferred Specification

Post-Father-Exit Outcomes (3+ Years Post)

Event-time estimates use a post-only design comparing children who experienced father exit to continuously-present controls, matched on birth year, sex, and race. These are near-term associations, not causal estimates.
